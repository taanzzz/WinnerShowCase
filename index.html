How Sessions Work

Simple to Enter, Rewarding to Win

Each session runs for exactly 15 minutes. Pick your direction, confirm your position — then watch the session unfold in real time. Results are calculated automatically when the clock hits zero.

  • Choose Your Direction
    Select Buy or Sell on your preferred asset. Set a lot size between 10 and 100 (in multiples of 10) and you're ready.
  • 15-Minute Countdown
    Every hour runs four back-to-back sessions. Watch live participation data update in real time as traders join from both sides.
  • Instant Results & Payout
    When the session closes, winners are determined automatically. Rewards arrive in your wallet right away — no delays, no manual processing.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you get started.

What is Winner App?
+
Winner App is a mobile trading competition platform where users participate in 15-minute sessions by choosing a market direction. At the end of each session, results are calculated automatically and winners receive their reward directly to their mobile wallet.
How do 15-minute sessions work?
+
Each hour has four back-to-back sessions running at :00, :15, :30, and :45. During a session, you pick a direction and set your position. When the countdown ends, results are calculated and winners are paid out immediately.
What is Team Trading?
+
Team Trading lets you trade alongside other users on a shared side — either Buy or Sell. All participants on the winning side share the reward. It's a social way to trade with a community behind you.
How do I deposit money?
+
Tap 'Deposit' on the home screen, select bKash, Nagad, or Rocket, and send money to the number shown. Copy your transaction ID and submit it in the app. Our team reviews and credits your wallet promptly.
What is the minimum position size?
+
The minimum lot size per trade is 10, and the maximum is 100. Position sizes must be in multiples of 10 (10, 20, 30... up to 100). Each lot corresponds to ৳10 in trade value.
What if a session ends in a tie?
+
If the session ends with equal participation on both sides, it's declared a tie. In this case, all participants receive their full position back. No one wins or loses — your funds are returned in full.
How does the badge system work?
+
Your badge reflects your cumulative trading volume. Starting as a Starter, you can progress through Verified, Trader, Pro Trader, Master, and finally Legend. Badges are displayed on your profile and show other traders how experienced you are.
Is Winner App available on iOS?
+
Winner App is currently available as an Android APK (Android 5.0 and above). An iOS version is in development. Stay tuned for announcements.
How does the referral system work?
+
Go to Profile, tap Refer & Earn, and use the 'Create Account for Friend' option. Enter their details and verify via OTP. Your referred users are linked to your account and appear in your referral list — along with their badge level.
Is my money safe on Winner App?
+
Yes. Winner App uses secure authentication, OTP verification, rate limiting, and a dual-balance system to protect your account and funds. Deposits and withdrawals are reviewed by our team, and all session results are processed server-side for complete integrity.
